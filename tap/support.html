Tap Support

Thank you for using Tap.

If you experience issues, find bugs, have suggestions, need help with your account, or would like to report a problem, please contact us:

tapapp@seznam.cz

Response Time

We aim to respond to support requests within 3–5 business days.

Common Topics

  • Account access and login issues
  • Friend requests and connections
  • Taps and mood taps
  • Push notification issues
  • Rewarded ad problems
  • Reporting or blocking users
  • Account deletion requests
  • Bug reports and feature suggestions

Account Deletion

Accounts can be deleted directly within the app through the Settings screen. If you experience any issues with account deletion, please contact us using the email address above.

App Information

Tap is a lightweight social app that allows friends to stay connected through simple taps and mood taps. Users can add friends, send taps, receive notifications, and manage their own notification preferences.
