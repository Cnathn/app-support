Tap

Simple by design. Just a tap.

Tap is a lightweight social app that helps friends and loved ones stay connected through simple taps, mood taps, and gentle notifications.

What You Can Do

  • Send a quick tap to a friend
  • Choose a mood tap when you want to say a little more
  • Add friends by username
  • Receive notifications when someone taps you
  • Manage notification preferences inside the app
  • Report or block users when needed

Privacy and Support

Tap includes account controls, notification preferences, reporting and blocking tools, and in-app account deletion.
