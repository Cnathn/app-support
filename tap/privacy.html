Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Tap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

When using Tap, we may collect:

  • Email address (when creating an account)
  • Username and profile information you provide
  • Push notification tokens used to deliver notifications
  • Friend connections and tap interactions between users
  • Technical information such as device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Advertising-related information necessary to display ads and reward users after ad views

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Allow users to connect with friends
  • Deliver taps, mood taps, and notifications
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Provide customer support
  • Serve advertisements and manage rewarded ad functionality

Advertising

Tap may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers. These providers may collect limited device information to deliver and measure ads in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Push Notifications

If enabled by the user, Tap may send push notifications related to taps, friend activity, invitations, and app updates. Users can disable notifications at any time through their device settings.

Data Storage

User data is stored using secure third-party infrastructure and databases. Reasonable measures are taken to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

Tap may use third-party services including:

  • Supabase (authentication and database services)
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)
  • Apple App Store and TestFlight services
  • Advertising providers such as Google AdMob

Children's Privacy

Tap is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
