Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Tap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

When using Tap, we may collect:

  • Email address when creating an account
  • Username and profile information you provide
  • Friend connections, friend requests, taps, mood taps, and related interactions
  • Push notification tokens used to deliver notifications
  • Technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, and basic diagnostic information
  • Advertising-related information necessary to display ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and reward users after rewarded ad views

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Allow users to connect with friends
  • Deliver taps, mood taps, friend requests, and notifications
  • Manage tap limits, cooldowns, and rewarded ad functionality
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Provide customer support

Account Information and Social Features

Tap is a social app. Your username, profile information, friend connections, taps, mood taps, and related activity may be visible to other users as part of the app's core functionality.

Advertising and Rewarded Ads

Tap may display rewarded advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob. Rewarded ads allow users to unlock extra app functionality, such as sending additional taps after reaching a cooldown or limit.

Advertising providers may collect and process limited device, usage, diagnostic, and advertising-related information to serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud or abuse, and comply with their own policies and legal obligations. Their collection and use of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

Push Notifications

If enabled by the user, Tap may send push notifications related to taps, mood taps, friend requests, reminders, achievements, and optional product updates. Users can disable notifications at any time through their device settings, and some notification preferences may also be managed inside the app.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored using secure third-party infrastructure and databases. Reasonable measures are taken to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

Tap may use third-party services including:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and backend services
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) for push notifications
  • Apple App Store and TestFlight services
  • Google AdMob or other advertising providers for rewarded ads

Children's Privacy

Tap is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights and Choices

You may delete your account at any time through the app's Settings screen. Deleting your account removes your account and associated personal data from Tap, except where limited information must be retained for security, legal, or operational reasons.

You may also contact us for privacy questions, account deletion assistance, or data-related inquiries using the email address below.
