Daily Deck Privacy Policy

Daily Deck is a vocabulary learning app that helps users learn new words through daily cards.

Data Stored on Your Device

Daily Deck stores your language preferences, learning progress, unlocked words, and notification preferences locally on your device.

No Account Required

Daily Deck does not require login, registration, or user accounts.

Notifications

Daily Deck may send optional local reminders if you enable notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Advertising

Daily Deck may use Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, diagnostics, and ad interaction data according to Google’s privacy policies.
